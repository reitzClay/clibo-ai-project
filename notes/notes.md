CLIBO AI: The Pip-Boy Inspired Life OSBridging the Gap between your Current State and your North Star.CLIBO is a passive, AI-integrated life management system designed to function like a real-world Pip-Boy. It lives in your workflow as an overlay, listening to your goals, observing your progress through "Proof of Evolution" media, and orchestrating a non-conflicting execution strategy across the seven pillars of human potential.🟢 The S.P.E.C.I.E.S. FrameworkCLIBO categorizes every action, goal, and "want" into seven core pillars:SocialPhysicalEmotionalCareerIntellectualEnvironmentalSpiritual🧠 Core Philosophy: The Calibration & The DeltaCLIBO isn't just a habit tracker; it's a Resource Allocator.Current State (The Anchor): You define where you are (e.g., Physical Speed: 5/10).The North Star (The Vision): You define where you want to be (e.g., Speed: 9/10).The Delta (The Gap): CLIBO calculates the effort required (+4) and the timeline (e.g., 9 months).Non-Overlapping Execution: CLIBO’s "Vagus Nerve" (AI Strategy Layer) audits your entire life timeline to schedule blocks of work that never overlap across different pillars.🛠 Technical ArchitectureBackend (Quarkus 3.35.2 + Java 21)Architecture: Domain-Driven Design (DDD) with a clean separation of Infrastructure and Domain.Intelligence: Integrated Llama 3.2:3b (via LangChain4j & Ollama) for multimodal intent extraction and strategy generation.Event-Driven: Kafka-backed messaging for the "Pulse" of the system.Persistence: PostgreSQL (Hibernate Panache) for Seeds (actions), VisionItems (goals), and MediaAssets (proof).Frontend (Flutter)Pip-Boy Overlay: A passive listener capable of capturing screenshots and chat to feed the AI.Horizontal Time Bar: A real-time, multi-track widget that visualizes your execution plan across all S.P.E.C.I.E.S.The Vault: A local-first media bucket for photos and videos that serve as "Proof of Evolution" for your Vision Board.🚀 Key FeaturesPassive Intent Capture: Take a screenshot of a gym plan or a code snippet; CLIBO extracts the "Want" and maps it to a strategy.Temporal Conflict Resolution: If a new Career goal clashes with your Physical training, CLIBO re-shuffles your timeline based on priority.Multimodal Vision Board: A living gallery of your progress, linking local media files directly to specific S.P.E.C.I.E.S. targets.Real-time Pulse: Live UI updates via WebSockets that react as the "Brain" processes your data.📁 Project Structuretextclibo-ai-project/
